Physical quantity: A quantity that can be measured by instrument, clearly defined and has proper units is called physical quantity. Physical quantities are classified as fundamental and derived quantities. Fundamental units: The physical quantity which does not depend on any other physical quantity is called a fundamental physical quantity such as length; mass and time are called fundamental units.
